Guidelines

Spawning in items

Admins and above are allowed to use the items menu to spawn in items within the lobby. This is strictly not for Moderators as they need to prove they can follow our rules before being promoted. If they can’t follow this rule, they can’t be trusted as an admin. Admins may spawn in anything from the items menu including fireworks but don't spawn in more than 1 or 2 at a time. Be reasonable, we're allowing this rule for you to have some fun and interact with the players. Abuse it, you lose it.

Admin Abuse

We all make mistakes and hit the wrong bind sometimes or forget to turn off god mode. If this happens to you, type sorry in chat to protect yourself from admin abuse claims as the chat logs will work as evidence in your favour. Staff found to have abused their powers will be blacklisted from admining on all servers and perm banned from Raid Simulator.

Flying around

If you are in Vanish and are flying around, you are still a physical object. Do not fly through gunfire in Vanish so you don't block bullets and rockets. Use debug camera instead to avoid interfering with the raid. Obviously you can still use Vanish while above a fight or while using /who.

Warnings

Raid Simulator is a chill server to hang out on when taking a break from, or preparing for the sweaty shitshow that is standard rust servers. PLEASE give warnings wherever possible. You will find that most people are actually very understanding if you are nice to them. Even if you don’t think they can speak English, try and warn them.

Etiquette

General regulations to follow:

  • Treat players and staff fairly and equally. Respect all members of staff and the community regardless of your opinion of them.

  • Do not have an attitude when dealing with players, you will only make the situation more difficult to settle.

  • Keep your opinions to staff chats - not in the public sector. This also includes declining suggestions, these are up to management to implement.

  • Be active in our staff discord and in game, your activity will be reviewed regularly by our Leadership team.

  • Anything said within staff channels does not leave staff channels.

  • Staff cannot enter VIP giveaways. Please do not react to the giveaway bot, if you win, it will be re-rolled regardless.

  • Your Discord and Battlemetrics names must be the same. Your steam name should also be the same although if you need to change it to, for example not get recognized on a main server, that’s fine.
